## Key Visual Observations
- **True Black Background**: Perfect for AMOLED screens.
- **Frosted Glass (Glassmorphism)**: Used for the search bar, movie cards, and the bottom dock.
- **Subtle Borders**: Thin white/grey borders (0.5dp) on glass elements to define shapes.
- **Vibrant Posters**: High-quality posters that pop against the black background.
- **Rounded Corners**: Consistent use of rounded corners (12dp - 20dp) for all UI elements.
- **Minimalist Icons**: Clean, white-tinted icons for navigation and actions.
